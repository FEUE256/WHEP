About WHEP

The White Hole Existence Principle (WHEP) is a theoretical framework developed by Nils Efverman and published by Förenta Evolutionernas Universitets Elektronik (FÈUE). WHEP investigates the properties and theoretical existence of white holes in the context of exotic matter and fundamental physics.

Document

The current working draft of WHEP is available as a PDF. It is a work-in-progress and subject to revisions.
